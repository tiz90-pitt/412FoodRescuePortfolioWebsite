Digital Storytelling Campaign: 412 Food Rescue

About The Project

ENGCMP610

This campaign shifts the perception of food rescue away from a purely logistical equation of "pounds and miles" and redefines it as a "living network" of human connection. By framing every individual food rescue as a "heartbeat," the campaign illustrates how these actions sustain the overall health of the Pittsburgh community.

The core conflict addresses a severe "logistics gap": 40% of food is wasted, yet one in seven residents faces daily hunger. Through strategic storytelling, visual design, and multimedia outreach, this portfolio piece aims to evolve the volunteer base from "transactional" app-based activism into a unified Advocate Network.
